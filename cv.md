## What I’m into
Fluid dynamics, optimization, and learning systems — I like models where math and reality meet.

**A personal favorite: the Navier–Stokes momentum equation**

$$
\left(\frac{\partial}{\partial t} + \mathbf{v}\cdot\nabla\right)\mathbf{v}
= -\frac{\nabla p}{\rho} + \nu \nabla^2\mathbf{v} + \mathbf{f}
$$
